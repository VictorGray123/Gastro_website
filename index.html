Traditional Spanish Dishes

Paella

A flavorful rice dish cooked with seafood, saffron, and vegetables, originating from Valencia.

Tortilla Española

A classic Spanish omelette made with eggs, potatoes, and onions. A hearty and popular dish.

Gazpacho

A chilled tomato-based soup filled with fresh vegetables, perfect for hot summer days.

Croquetas

Fried breaded rolls filled with creamy béchamel sauce mixed with ham or chicken.

Jamón Ibérico

The finest cured ham in Spain, made from free-range Iberian pigs.

Patatas Bravas

Crispy fried potatoes served with a spicy tomato sauce and aioli.

Churros

Fried dough pastry, often served with hot chocolate for dipping. A popular breakfast treat.

Fabada Asturiana

A rich bean stew with chorizo, morcilla (blood sausage), and pork shoulder.

Pimientos de Padrón

Small green peppers, typically fried and sprinkled with sea salt. Some are spicy, others mild.

Pulpo a la Gallega

Tender octopus, served with paprika, olive oil, and sea salt, hailing from Galicia.
